Atlanta Community Food Bank

Our mission is to fight hunger by engaging, educating and empowering our community. While our core work is food distribution, our efforts extend far beyond that. Our mission is lived out every day by engaging, educating and empowering both people in need and those who want to help. From volunteering your time to assisting people in finding economic security, the Food Bank covers a wide range of opportunities for people to learn and get involved.

Feeding GA Families

Feeding GA Families provides food, temporary housing, and basic community resources to people in need. Our vision is to lower food insecurity within our communities, improve individual and family wellbeing, and provide healthier food options to at risk populations, children, low-income households, medically at-risk, seniors, unhoused (homeless), and veterans by making food access more readily available, assisting with access to basic resource opportunities, and offering safe, nutritious food to all clients with health or dietary restrictions.

Open Hand Atlanta

Open Hand Atlanta is a meals-on-wheels program for individuals living with AIDS/HIV, critical illnesses such as cancer, diabetes, or disabilities such as spinal cord injuries and sight loss, as well as senior citizens in the Atlanta metropolitan area. We prepare, package, and deliver over 6,000 meals daily.
